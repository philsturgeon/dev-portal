## FAQs

**How do I access these APIs?**

Our APIs are available for all users of the Street software. If you are a current user and need an access token, please contact support or follow the guide available on our [help pages](https://intercom.help/streetcouk/en/articles/8474378-generating-an-api-token).

**How do I get notified of any changes or updates?**

We will be sending out regular emails and updating our Slack channels when we release any new features or changes to the APIs

**Is it possible to make feature requests?**

Please do! Feel free to [submit a ticket](http://api-support.street.co.uk/) and we'll check it out!
