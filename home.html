FAQs

How do I access these APIs?

Our APIs are available for all users of the Street software. If you are a current user and need an access token, please contact support or follow the guide available on our help pages.

How do I get notified of any changes or updates?

We will be sending out regular emails and updating our Slack channels when we release any new features or changes to the APIs

Is it possible to make feature requests?

Please do! Feel free to submit a ticket and we'll check it out!
